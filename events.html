Asian Pacific American Heritage Month Celebration
This is a Music, Dance, Culture & Community, Fairs, film & Family event in which tributes are paid to the generations of Asian and Pacific Islanders who have enriched America's history. DCA celebrates Asian Pacific American Heritage Month with our annual Calendar and Cultural Guide presenting virtual events through May and beyond that honor LA’s Asian Pacific American residents, visitors, artists, and communities. The Starford Festival: 'Macbeth'
An enriching theatre experience brought alive by the world's finest talents. Surrender to a haunting story of ambition and its dark consequences, as a military hero and his wife conspire to seize the throne of Scotland. The Stratford Festival is a sensational theatre festival which runs between the months of April and October. In Los Angeles, it was held on May 30, from noon to midnight, this year.
